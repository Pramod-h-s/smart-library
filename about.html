About Smart Library

Vision & Mission

Vision: To create a smart and efficient digital library ecosystem that enhances accessibility, transparency, and user experience for students and administrators.

Mission: To simplify library operations with real-time book tracking, digital inventory, and seamless issuing & returning workflows.

Project Overview

Smart Library is a digital library management solution designed to simplify book issuing, returning, and inventory monitoring. Built with a clean and intuitive interface, it supports barcode scanning, CSV import/export, and real-time book tracking.


Our goal is to modernize library workflows through efficient, scalable, and user-friendly software.
